🏸 VR BADMINTON

A full badminton match in VR for Meta Quest. Hold a real racket — the game scans your hand and overlays a ghost racket that tracks 1:1.

  • • Put on your Quest headset and open this page in the Quest Browser.
  • • Tap ENTER VR, allow hand tracking when asked.
  • • Reach out and pinch / trigger the floating READY FOR THE SHOT button.
  • • Swing to hit the shuttle over the net. First to 7 points wins.
No external game assets — everything is generated procedurally.
