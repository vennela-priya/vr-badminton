🏸 VR BADMINTON

A full badminton match in VR for Meta Quest. A glowing virtual racket tracks your hand 1:1.

  • • Put on your Quest headset and open this page in the Quest Browser.
  • • Tap ENTER VR, allow hand tracking when asked.

  • • Swing to hit the shuttle over the net. First to 7 points wins.
